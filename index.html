About Good Life Music

Good Life Music (GLM) is a record label based in Athens, GA. What started with a dream was founded in the hopes of becoming the most trusted brand in music.

In an environment where labels are often seen as anything but trusted, GLM focuses on healthy artist relationships and quality output. Made up of a team of UGA students, GLM understands that healthy, well-supported musicians make the best music.

Built on hard work and a mission, GLM is ready to bring great music and real change to the music industry.
