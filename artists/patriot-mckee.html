Patriot McKee is a Christian rapper from Atlanta, Georgia. After releasing his first album at 14, Patriot found a love for music that brings people hope through encouragement and shared experiences, from sharing testimonies to sharing vibes. Patriot moves fluidly between styles, maintaining a straightforward and lyrical approach to lyrics and delivery while exploring where different sounds and flows suit his taste. Patriot knows that he is never a finished product, nor is his music, and he invites you to join him in the development and discovery of both.
